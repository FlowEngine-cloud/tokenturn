# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Tokenturn to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience, background, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and reviews.
- Assume good intent and ask before assuming the worst.
- Critique code and ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, code, issues, and PRs that violate
this code, and may block repeat offenders. Report concerns privately to
**hi@flowengine.cloud**; reports are handled confidentially.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the spirit of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
